Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a headshot session take?

A standard session typically runs 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfit changes. Executive sessions can extend to 60 minutes for additional variety.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and classic business attire. Bring 2-3 outfit options to vary your looks. We'll provide detailed guidance during your pre-session consultation.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll see your images in an online gallery within 48 hours. After selecting your favorites, retouched final images are delivered within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional makeup artists specializing in camera-ready looks. This service can be added to any package for an additional fee.

What about retouching and editing?

Professional retouching is included with every headshot. We ensure natural-looking results while removing temporary blemishes and minor distractions.
